# Tidefall: Dead Current — Design Document

## Core Fantasy

Leave the safe harbor, sail into the Dead Current, fight AI and players, loot islands, and extract safely to bank your rewards.

## Pillars

1. **Compact Open Archipelago** — islands are close enough to encourage encounters but far enough that travel matters.
2. **PvPvE** — AI creates danger and reveals player activity.
3. **Loot and Theft** — death drops a backpack; boats have lootable cargo.
4. **Crafting Guns** — data-driven recipes and blueprints affect playstyle.
5. **Tactical Shooter Combat** — recoil, ADS, headshots, movement inaccuracy.

## Player Loop

1. Spawn at The Haven
2. Craft / equip / accept contract
3. Spawn skiff and sail
4. Land on island, fight AI, loot
5. Encounter or avoid players
6. Extract to Haven and bank
7. Craft improved gear

## Progression

- Currency: Scrap
- Factions: Freeport Compact, Salvagers Guild, Wardens, Black Flag Syndicate
- Blueprints unlock better recipes
- Reputation gates future contracts
- Progression unlocks options, not massive power

## World

2200 x 2200 unit circular sea bounded by a storm wall. Safe hub at center. Six distinct locations.

## Death Rules

Inside safe zone: no damage, no theft. Outside safe zone: full PvP, dropped backpack is free loot, boat cargo lootable. Respawn at Haven with starter gear; retain banked items, blueprints, and reputation.
